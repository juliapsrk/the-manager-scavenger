The Manager Scavenger

(A passive-aggressive game)

For people who love their bosses and corporate jobs

This Is You:




You're currently stuck in your 9-5 job that slowly but certainly kills
you on the inside. You are being attacked by an endless number
of incoming emails and zoom calls, as well as your manager themselves.



So, here's your chance to release some frustration!

Instructions

Protect yourself by avoiding them and/or by shooting at them
with the little f$%*s (strikes) you have left to give.

Collect to load up on strikes and to increase your score.
Watch out for the bombs!

Controls

Use the arrow-keys to move the player in each horizontal and vertical
direction. Use the space bar to shoot.

and you're still stuck in your 9-5 job.
