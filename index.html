The Manager Scavenger

A passive-aggressive game for people who
love their bosses and corporate jobs

Instructions

You're stuck in your 9-5 job and need a break.
Fight off the obstacles (emails, zoom calls, your boss)
and collect powerUps (batteries and vacations) to not
get stuck in the corporate rat race.

You Lost

You're still stuck in your 9-5 job.

Controls

Use the arrow-keys to move the player in each horizontal and vertical direction
Use the space bar to shoot
