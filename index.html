The Manager Scavenger

(A passive-aggressive game)

For people who love their bosses and corporate jobs

The Game

This is you:
You're currently stuck in your 9-5 job that slowly but certainly kills you on the inside. You are being attacked by an endless number of incoming emails and zoom calls, as well as your manager themselves.


So, here's your chance to release some frustration!

You may have killed off something wrong if you can read this enemies, but still:

You Lost

and you're still stuck in your 9-5 job.

To get a chance at obtaining freedom:

Instructions

Protect yourself by avoiding them and/or by shooting at them with the little f$%*s (strikes) you have left to give.

Your helpers:
Collect: to load up on strikes.
Collect: to increase your score.

Watch out for the bombs!

Controls

Use the arrow-keys to move the player in each horizontal and vertical direction. Use the space bar to shoot.
